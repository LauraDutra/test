Gender Equity Training

​Uplift gender diversity within your organization.

​We offer workshops and trainings for organizations and professionals who want to provide an inclusive environment for individuals of all genders.

Nonprofits, healthcare providers, educational institutions, religious organizations, and tech companies trust us to lead them on the path towards equity.​

Whether your organization is just beginning its gender diversity journey, or is a seasoned ally, we have workshops to help you meet your inclusion needs.

Build a better workplace.

Adults in the US spend about one-third of their lives at work. For trans and nonbinary people, this can be an added source of stress and anxiety. Gender expansive folks deserve to feel like they can show up as themselves without the added stress of microaggressions, harassment, or discrimination. 
​
Working with TACT can help your organization build a better workplace for people of all genders. We can help your team lay the foundations for your organization to be a more values-aligned, psychologically safe environment. By building a better workplace for trans and nonbinary folks, you build a better workplace for everyone! 

Trusted by

Our approach is nonjudgmental, supportive, and interactive.

We understand how intimidating it can be to learn new concepts and unlearn old habits, which is why our approach is nonjudgmental, supportive, and interactive. We want you to feel comfortable asking your genuine questions without fear of saying the wrong thing. 

Our training programs center trans joy. The challenges facing trans and nonbinary individuals are only part of the story. Each of our workshops is an invitation for you to participate in our joy with us and carry it forward into your workplace. 
​
Together, we can create working environments where trans and nonbinary people thrive. 

Our workshops:

Gender 101

A 2-hour intensive training on gender inclusive practices in the workplace that introduces learners to the basics of gender identity and gender diversity.​

Training for Supervisors

An in-depth training on supporting your trans and nonbinary employees for supervisors and mangers.​

Organizational Change

A 90-minute collaborative session where we assess how supportive your organization is currently for trans and nonbinary individuals and develop action steps to address inclusion gaps.
If you're ready to get started or receive a training quote from us, please fill out our Interest Form below.

​If you don’t see an option that quite fits your organization’s need, get in touch so we can create something just for you. 

Consulting Services

We also provide long and short term consulting services for organizations to create and implement gender equity policies and practices. Our goal is to ensure that individuals across the gender spectrum feel safe and respected in a variety of organizations and institutions.
