We believe communit​y care is the answer.
Everyone can thrive when they have a sense of belonging and gender congruence.
​
Our Gender Advocates are trans-identified peer counselors who provide compassionate listening and affirming resources to trans and nonbinary folks who are looking for support.

Trans joy is possible! 
Isolation, stigma, lack of support networks, and barriers to care are what prevent so many trans folks from accessing joy and wellbeing.
​
We believe that trans joy is not only a right: it is a tangible possibility.

Let's build a world where trans folks can thrive. 
We know that trans folks can live vibrant, happy lives when they have the support and connection they need. We believe that by providing a virtual network of peer support we can improve trans individuals' overall wellbeing by reducing isolation and barriers to care.
​
We are committed to making our world a place where trans folks can thrive. 

What we offer

Peer Counseling with a Gender Advocate
Gender Advocates provide free and sliding-scale peer counseling for trans and nonbinary adults across the US.​
